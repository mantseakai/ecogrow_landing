Pioneering Sustainable Agriculture in Ghana

EcoGrow Farms is building Ghana's premier circular economy farm in Gomoa Abotsia, Central Region. We integrate crop production, livestock management, aquaculture, and commercial feed manufacturing into one harmonious ecosystem.

Our 100-acre operation demonstrates that profitability and environmental stewardship go hand-in-hand, creating economic value while protecting our environment for future generations.
